--- DEPENDENCIES
- xorg
    -- libxinerama
    -- libxft
    -- xorg-server
    -- xorg-xinit (if no Display Manager)

- brightnessctl (monitor & kbd brightness control)

- pcmanfm (file manager, not required)

- pulseaudio
    -- pavucontrol (GUI volume control)
    -- pactl (CLI volume control)
    -- plauerctl (required by the spotify script)

- xautolock (slock)

- picom-tryone-git (dual_kawase blur method) 

--- FONTS

- UbuntuMono Nerd Font (`nerd-fonts-ubuntu-mono` in the AUR)
- Material Design Icons (`ttf-material-design-icons` in the standard Arch repo
- A font that supports japanese chars (noto CJK jp `https://www.google.com/get/noto/#sans-jpan` or any other)
